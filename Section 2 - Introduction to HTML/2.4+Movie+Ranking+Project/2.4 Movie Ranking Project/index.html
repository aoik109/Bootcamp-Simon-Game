The Best Movies According to Allison

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spiderman: Across the Spider-Verse

Breathtaking art with a wide variety of artstyles. The big chase with all the spider people was INSANE.

Captain America: The First Avenger

Amazing plotline and characters. The action scenes were great! I loved seeing how Chris Evans became Captain America.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

Best humor. I was laughing the whole time. Got to see Rocket's backstory
